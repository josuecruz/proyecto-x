## About Slingshot 2026

Slingshot is Singapore's flagship deep tech startup pitching competition, now in its 10th edition. Organized by SGInnovate and hosted on Agorize, it brings together 6,800+ startups globally. Top 50 Global Startups pitch live at SWITCH (Singapore Week of Innovation and Technology).

### Key Details
- **Type:** Global deep tech startup pitching competition
- **Focus:** Deep tech — AI, fintech, healthtech, sustainability, etc.
- **Prize Pool:** Over S$2.2M (~US$1.65M) in grant prizes
  - Grand Winner: S$400,000 Startup SG Grant Prize
  - 1st Runner Up: S$300,000 Startup SG Grant Prize
  - 2nd Runner Up: S$200,000 Startup SG Grant Prize
  - Other finalist prizes available
- **What to submit:** Pitch deck (maximum 10 slides)
- **Final event:** Live pitching at SWITCH 2026 in Singapore (Oct 27–29)
- **One founder/co-founder sponsored** for travel to finals

### Eligibility
- Has an MVP (Minimum Viable Product)
- Registered within the past 10 years in country of registration
- Not a subsidiary of a corporate entity
- Any geography (global)

1. **What is your innovation?** (250 words max)

Our core innovation is a proprietary deep learning system that scores creditworthiness from alternative data sources — utility payments, mobile usage patterns, behavioral signals, and remittance flows — delivering credit decisions in under 5 seconds at <$5 cost per loan. Unlike existing fintechs that bolt AI onto legacy architecture from US/European lenders, our entire stack was built AI-first from day one: graph neural networks for fraud detection, transformers for document understanding, and reinforcement learning for dynamic risk pricing. And 50% of the economy operates informally — no pay stubs, no tax returns, no credit score. This isn't just a market inefficiency; it's a structural barrier that traps millions in poverty. We solve this with an AI-native architecture that can score creditworthiness from alternative data sources not used by traditional credit bureaus."
