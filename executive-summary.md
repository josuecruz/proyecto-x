Mexico's financial system excludes 60%+ of adults from formal credit. Traditional banks reject 7/10 SME applications. Incumbent fintechs bolt AI onto legacy architectures; none were built AI-first from day one.

### Solution
AI-native platform using proprietary deep learning models for credit scoring (alternative data), fraud detection (real-time), and hyper-personalized financial products. Our stack processes decisions in <5 seconds using thin-file data — no бюрократия, no branches.
